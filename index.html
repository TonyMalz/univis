Montag 4.7.2016
 17:00 - 22:00 Uhr Bamberg Buddies' Independence Day Barbecue Hainstr 41-43 Professur für Amerikanistik

To facilitate transatlantic exchange, we welcome all interested students to join the Bamberg Buddies Program. Take this opportunity to make new international friends and spend a fun afternoon and evening with American, Canadian, German and other students. This month, you are invited to our annual Bamberg Buddies Barbecue in the Hainpark.

On July 4th, at 5 p.m. we will meet at the Bayerleinswiese (Hainstr 41-43, see map below!) for a fun barbecue-potluck-picnic.
The idea is that everyone will contribute something to our barbecue:
we bring the grill - you bring your own meat/ alternative barbecue fare (because tastes are different) we bring barbecue sauces, bread, beer and water - you bring sides, salads, desserts, and other drinks (because we like diversity) we bring games - you bring your own cups and plates (because we care about the environment).

In order to help us calculate, please sign up on our foodlist or send an email to bambergbuddies.amerikanistik(at)uni-bamberg.de.

We will organize an alternative venue in case it rains. Please check our website and Facebook for updates.
We - the American Studies Team and the Team of the Englische Sprachpraxis - are looking forward to seeing you all on July 4th!

Kontakt: Spychala, Mareike
Professur für Amerikanistik
Telefon 2241, E-Mail: mareike.spychala@uni-bamberg.de
